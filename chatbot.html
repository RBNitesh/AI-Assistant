OUR LEADERS

Dr. Ravuri Venkataswamy

Chairman

Dr. R. Venkataswamy, S/o. late Sri R. Kanakaiah is a well known Educationist and Philanthropist of this region. With a keen interest to promote education in the backward Rayalaseema Region of Andhra Pradesh, with a motto of “Education for Peace” he has floated an educational society in the name of “Srinivasa Educational Academy”.

R.V. Srinivas

Vice Chairman

Shri R.V. Srinivas, B.Tech., MBA (UK) is the Vice-Chairman for Srinivasa Educational Academy and playing a proactive role in the management of following 23 Educational Institutions. He did his MBA from CARDIFF University, Wales (7th Best University in U.K.) specializing in Finance and Marketing.

Dr. Matam Mohan Babu

Principal

Dr. Matam Mohan Babu, an eminent academician and administrator, serves as the Principal and Professor of Civil Engineering at Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering & Technology (Autonomous), Chittoor. With over 27 years of experience, he has contributed immensely to academia, research, and institutional development.
