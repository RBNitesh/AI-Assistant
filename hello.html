Admissions

Explore eligibility, important dates, and everything you need to know to start your journey with us.

Eligibility Criteria

  • For B.Tech: 10+2 with PCM, min 45% (General), 40% (Reserved)
  • For MBA: Any UG degree with min 50% (General), 45% (Reserved)
  • Entrance exams: EAMCET, ICET, GATE, etc.

Programs Offered

  • B.Tech – CSE, ECE, EEE, Mech, Civil
  • M.Tech – CSE, Embedded Systems, Power Electronics
  • MBA – Finance, HR, Marketing
  • Diploma – Mechanical, EEE

Admission Process

  1. Apply online/offline through college portal or counseling center.
  2. Appear for entrance exam (as applicable).
  3. Submit documents for verification.
  4. Pay admission fee to confirm your seat.

Documents Required

  • 10th & 12th Mark Sheets
  • Transfer Certificate (TC)
  • Entrance Exam Rank Card
  • Caste & Income Certificate (if applicable)
  • Aadhaar Card, Passport-size photos

Important Dates

  • Application Opens: May 1st
  • Entrance Exams: May–June
  • Counseling & Seat Allotment: July
  • Class Commencement: August

FAQs

Can I apply without an entrance exam?

Yes, management quota seats are available for direct admission. Contact our admission office for more info.

Is there a hostel facility for first-year students?

Yes, separate hostel facilities are available for boys and girls with strict security and good amenities.
